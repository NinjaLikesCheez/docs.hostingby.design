title: Can I upgrade my disk space?
sidebar_label: Diskspace upgrade
---
Yes, naturally!
Login to your client area, click on your service, once you are there you will see the following options:
![Client Panel](http://i.imgur.com/EMe1z0x.png)

At the left side, click Upgrade/Downgrade Options and you will get the options below:
![Upgrade](http://i.imgur.com/hYzrq6L.png)

Do note, that from here on out, the upgrade process is 100% automatic, you will get an invoice for the remaining period, once that is paid the upgrade is done within 10 minutes.

We support a very large range of payment options. We have listed them all below, including the fees for each method.

- PayPal (Fee: None!)

- VISA/MasterCard (Fee: None!)

- Bitcoin / LiteCoin / Ethereum (Fee: None!)

- iDeal (Fee: 5%)

- EPS (Fee: 5%)

- Neosurf (Fee: 7.5%)

We are always looking for new ways to make it easier for our customers, don't hessitate to contact us about a new way to receive payments.
